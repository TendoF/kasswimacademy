About KAS Swim Academy

Where Champions
Are Made

KAS Swim Academy is Harare's dedicated aquatic training hub, based at 108 Greendale Avenue, Greendale. We offer professional swimming lessons and triathlon training for kids and adults — from complete beginners through to competitive athletes.

Our athletes have competed at the Bonaqua Africa Triathlon Cup and represented Triathlon Zimbabwe at the SA Youth Championships. We develop not just swimmers, but complete, confident, competitive athletes.

Triathlon Training

KAS Academy offers structured triathlon training covering all three disciplines — Swim, Bike, and Run — for ages 4 and above. Our athletes have competed at the Bonaqua Africa Triathlon Cup and the SA Youth Championships, with 5 medal winners from the academy in 2025.

Track Record

Real Results,
Real Champions

KAS athletes don't just train — they compete and win. Our academy has a proud record of producing competitive swimmers and triathletes who represent Zimbabwe at regional and continental level.

Bonaqua Africa Triathlon Cup — Troutbeck, 202518 KAS athletes competed. 5 medal winners: Anodiwa Mushamba, Shailyn Museka, Munenyasha Vambe, Ethan Chinamasa & Jayden Mverechena.
SA Youth Triathlon Championships — EbotseKAS athletes qualified to represent Triathlon Zimbabwe at the SA Youth Championships.
Open Water AchievementYoungest swimmers Yemurai Chiwaka & Mudiwa Chinamasa completed the 250m open water course at the Africa Triathlon Cup.
